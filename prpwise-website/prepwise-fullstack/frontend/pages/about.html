Privacy Policy

PrepWise takes your privacy seriously. All video and audio analysis runs client-side in your browser using WebAssembly — raw video frames are never uploaded to our servers. Only aggregated performance scores are stored in your account.

We do not sell your data to third parties. Your phone number is used only for authentication. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting support.

Terms of Service

By using PrepWise you agree to use the platform for lawful, personal interview preparation purposes only. You may not attempt to reverse-engineer, scrape, or misuse the AI systems. PrepWise is provided "as is" and we make no guarantees about job placement outcomes.

Cookie Policy

PrepWise uses only essential cookies for authentication session management (JWT tokens stored in localStorage). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You can clear your session data at any time from your browser settings.
