Top Interview Tips

The most impactful habits our highest-scoring users share.

01
Use STAR Every Time
Situation, Task, Action, Result. Every behavioral question should follow this structure. Interviewers are trained to look for it.
02
Look at the Camera
Look at the lens, not your own face on screen. It registers as direct eye contact to the interviewer and reads as confidence.
03
Pause Before Answering
2-3 seconds of silence signals thoughtfulness. Most candidates jump to answer immediately — a brief pause makes you stand out.
04
Add Numbers Always
"I improved performance" is weak. "I reduced load time by 40%, serving 2M users" is memorable. Quantify every achievement you mention.
